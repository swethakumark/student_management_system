    • Botanical Garden

  • The campus of Government College, Madappally was a barren land till 1982 when, the nature loving teachers and students of the college decided to initiate the process of aforestation of the hillock. As a first step a nature club was registered with World Wide Fund for Nature (WWF), Trivandrum (Register No.S-KER/848 dated 13.10.1982). The following years witnessed the transformation of the barren, rocky landscape into a lush green campus garden.

    • NCC

  • Government College Madappally has an active unit of NCC which functions to develop character comradeship and the capacity for leadership among the students. It provides service training to its members in order to stimulate interest in the defence of the Nation. The NCC cadets are actively involved in the social service activities around the locality. The NCC unit of the college makes its presence felt through representations in the annual Republic Day parade.
